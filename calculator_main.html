Welcome to the Geospatial Research Institute's Methane Emissions Calculator!

This tool is designed to estimate methane (CH4) emissions from livestock using the IPCC (universal) and MPI (NZ-specific) methodologies with an easy-to-use and comprehensive User Interface and full transparency on equations, defaults values used and easy handling of recurring data. It supports Tier 1 and Tier 2 calculations for various categories of livestock and includes manure management contributions.
A Tier 3 methodology is under development, stay tuned for updates!

Both IPCC and MPI offer detailed documents describing their methodologies (see the References section below), however due to the extensive nature of these documentation, users can refer to the corresponding working paper as well as the equations section for an overview of all equations used in the respective emissions estimations.

See the tutorial to follow through each section and the possible user entries.
